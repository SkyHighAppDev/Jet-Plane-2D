How To Move The Plane

Click the arrow keys at the bottom of the key pad.





The Airport

The airport is for landing the plane.





Storms

If you strike a storm, your aircraft will be thrown around in a down draft. Upsets passengers. Large storms will destroy your aircraft.





Canada Geese

If a Canada Goose hits your aircraft the plane will take somewhat minor damage and make passengers upset





Other Aircraft

The other aircraft may collide with your aircraft. The collision will destroy your aircraft.

Missile

Missiles are hard to get around because they are fast. May destroy or cause major damage and make passengers upset.
