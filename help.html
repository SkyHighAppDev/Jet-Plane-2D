How To Fly The Plane

Click the arrow keys at the bottom of the key pad.





The Airport

This is where you land your airplane.





Storms

If you strike a storm, your aircraft will be thrown around in a down draft. Upsets passengers. Large storms can fatal.





Canada Geese

Bird strike! Watch out for Canada Geese. They appear soft but they can destroy your engines and upset passengers.





Missile

Missiles are fast moving. Avoiding them is challenging. These will destroy your airplane.
